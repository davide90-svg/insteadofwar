The numbers on the main dashboard of *Instead Of War* are staggering. But behind those billions of dollars lies a deeper structural issue.

## A systemic problem
When a nation decides to increase its military budget, that money doesn't appear out of thin air. It is actively diverted from social infrastructure.

### What we found today
According to the latest reports, if we stopped spending on weapons for just one week, we could:
* Build hundreds of hospitals.
* End world hunger for a month.

> "Every gun that is made signifies a theft from those who hunger."
